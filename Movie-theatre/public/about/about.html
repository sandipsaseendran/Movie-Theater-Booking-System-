Since 2015, Nilambur Films has been Kerala's premier destination for world-class cinema experiences, blending cutting-edge technology with unmatched hospitality.

OUR JOURNEY

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in the heart of Nilambur, our vision was simple yet ambitious: to create a cinema experience that transcends traditional movie-watching. We dreamed of a place where technology, comfort, and artistry converge to deliver unforgettable moments.

Today, with 12 premium screens across our flagship location, we serve over 150,000 happy guests annually. Each screen is equipped with state-of-the-art laser projection, Dolby Atmos sound, and luxury seating that redefines comfort.
